About

Hiya, The name's Lya

I'm a passionate Computer Science student and developer with a specialty in front-end web development.

I am currently pursuing a degree in Information Software for Organisations (SIO), specializing in Software Solutions and Work Applications (SLAM).

To see the technologies I work with, you can check out the My Work section of this website (the bottom left button).

In my free time, I enjoy diving into new tech, creating 3D models, and exploring new technologies. I plan on specializing in front-end development as I continue my studies, while also investing time in 3D modeling to collaborate with others in the field.

I have plenty of hobbies, but my top 5 would be:

  • Coding (Front-end focus)
  • Watching Anime
  • Playing Story-driven Games
  • Reading Science Fiction Novels (Isaac Asimov my beloved <3)
